Single Tab Limit (Domain Rules)
Forces a website to stay in a single tab. Opening any page on the site closes and redirects you to the existing tab.
Example: If youtube.com/watch?v=123 is open, opening youtube.com/watch?v=456 closes the new tab and switches you back.
▼
Specific Page Deduplication (Exact Rules)
Deduplicates only this exact specific URL.
Example: Only closes duplicates of reddit.com/r/news, but allows reddit.com/r/funny to stay open.
▼
Site-Specific Page Deduplication (Path Rules)
Prevents duplicate tabs of identical pages across this website, while allowing different pages to coexist.
Example: Closes duplicate tabs of github.com/issues/123, but lets github.com/issues/456 stay open.
